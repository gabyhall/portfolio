My Journey to Code Nation

I have spent time as a translator, having studied foreign languages at university, and as a researcher studying innovation in high-tech sectors. Coding offered me the opportunity to learn something new in a way that uses some of the same skills as learning a new language, but with more logic and fewer exceptions to the rules! With Code Nation I am looking forward to forging a career in tech, which I had previously thought I could only research from the outside looking in. I am excited to see where coding could take me.
